Welcome to the digital gateway of With God Private Nursery and Primary School! Nestled in the heart of our community, our school stands as a beacon of excellence in education, guided by a profound commitment to nurturing young hearts and minds. At With God Private, we believe in the transformative power of education grounded in faith. Our curriculum is designed to not only impart academic knowledge but also instill moral values, integrity, and compassion. With a dedicated team of experienced educators and staff, we create a safe and supportive learning environment where each child is encouraged to explore, discover, and grow. From our vibrant nursery program, where curiosity is sparked and imaginations run wild, to our dynamic primary school curriculum, where foundations are laid for a lifetime of learning, every step of the educational journey at With God Private is crafted with care and intentionality. Beyond academics, we offer a rich tapestry of extracurricular activities, ranging from sports and arts to community service initiatives, ensuring that each child has the opportunity to explore their interests and talents.
